About the Tool — How It Works

⚡

Internet speed test

Amilma Network measures the real speed of your internet connection using Cloudflare CDN infrastructure deployed across more than 300 locations worldwide. You get precise download and upload speed values in Mbps, as well as ping latency and jitter stability — all in one test that takes less than 30 seconds.

◆

IP address & ISP check

We automatically detect your public IP address, internet service provider (ISP) and geographic location as soon as the page loads. No registration, no tracking cookies. Ideal for verifying your VPN connection, diagnosing network problems, or simply finding out who provides your internet service.

◆

Gaming & streaming assessment

Based on the measured values, the tool automatically assesses your connection's suitability for competitive FPS gaming (ping <35 ms, jitter <10 ms), voice chat apps like Discord and Teams, and livestreaming on platforms like Twitch and YouTube. You get a clear rating: Excellent, Stable, or Poor.
